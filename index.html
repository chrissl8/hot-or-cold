What do I do?

This is how it works: we guess a number between 1 and 100, and you try to figure it out!

  • 1. First, we pick a random number between 1 to 100 and keep it hidden from you.
  • 2. You'll need to keep guessing until you can guess this hidden secret number.
  • 3. To help you out, we'll give you feedback on how hot or cold you are, and whether you're too high or too low.
